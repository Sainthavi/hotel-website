About LOTUS five star hotel

In a sleek high-rise building overlooking the Laccadive Sea, this upmarket hotel is 4 minutes' walk from Liberty Plaza mall and 35 km from Bandaranaike International Airport. Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows with sea or city views, the chic rooms provide free Wi-Fi, flat-screens, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms have Nespresso machines and access to a private lounge. Suites add living areas. Room service is available. There's a buffet eatery, plus French and Japanese restaurants. A rooftop terrace has an infinity pool, a hot tub, a cocktail bar and sea views. Other amenities include a spa and a gym.
